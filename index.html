Things I love doing

Lead generation

The initiation of consumer interest is a key first step in an effective acquisition process. Whether it is about winning your first customers or fine-tuning an existing strategy, I'm here to help: identify high-potential lead generation channels for your brand, set performance targets, create and optimize campaigns. Areas I have experience with include social media ads, email marketing, content marketing, SEA and display ads. Whenever needed, campaigns will be supported by a new, conversion-optimized traffic destination: individual landing pages or a custom website.

Inbound marketing funnels

Moving leads from the top of the marketing funnel through to becoming sales-ready leads or customers often requires a multi-step nurturing process. Depending on your specific market, this may include retargeting ads, email newsletters, webinars, blog articles, downloadable content, individual discount codes, dedicated landing pages and more. When designing an inbound marketing funnel, I strive for the highest level of trackability, automation and audience segmentation. Needless to say, the process will be integrated with your CRM software (i.a. Hubspot, Pipedrive).

Social media strategy

Social media offers plenty of ways to connect with your audience, transform your brand image, showcase your products and generate sales. I help brands develop and execute social media strategies that cover all stages of the marketing funnel, from Awareness and Consideration to Purchase and Advocacy. Whether your goal is to grow your audience, generate leads, or build a strong online community, I have solutions to offer: development and optimization of ad campaigns, creation of visual & written content, community management or influencer marketing. Once it is time to scale, count on me for building and managing an internal or exteral team of experts who will bring your social media game to the next level. Platforms I have worked with: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube and TikTok.

Influencer marketing

By 2020, Influencer Marketing has become an integral part of the marketing mix for a tremendous number of B2C and B2B brands alike. It allows brands to generate awareness quickly, boost sales, create a whole new level of engagement with their target customers, and tap into new audiences. Yet up until now, many aspects of influencer partnerships often remain intransparent and hardly measurable, while some brands have even fallen victim to influencer fraud. With 5 years of experience in the field, I promote transparent and data-driven approach to influencer collaborations with a strong focus on brand fit, audience quality, and in-depth analytics. My exlertise lies in influencer identification, matching, negotiation & contracting, campaign management and quality assurance.
